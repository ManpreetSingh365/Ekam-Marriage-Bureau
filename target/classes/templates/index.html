About Us

Ekam Matchmaker was founded in 2001 by Madam Baljit Kaur, a respected professional teacher who turned her passion for bringing hearts together into a full-time mission. What began with a fond interest in helping families find the right match has grown into a trusted name in community matchmaking.

Driven by her dedication, warmth, and deep understanding of relationships, Madam Baljit Kaur transitioned fully into this field, pouring her heart and soul into building a platform rooted in trust, respect, and love.

Our Promise

We believe that every match is a story waiting to unfold. Our approach is personal, heartfelt, and focused on understanding the values, dreams, and expectations of every individual we work with.
